What Is It

Not a lecture. Not a conference.

Sandbox Sessions is a small cohort coaching series for practitioners who want to troubleshoot real problems, think differently, and get better at the craft.

You bring a real problem. The room works it. Facilitated, peer-led, honest.

Small Cohort Problems-First Peer-Led No Slides Open to All Coaches
